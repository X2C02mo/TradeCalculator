Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data Trader Calculator (Telegram Mini App) processes and why. The app is designed to work without requiring you to enter personal data.

1) What we collect

  • Inputs you type (prices, risk, etc.) are processed locally in your browser to calculate results.
  • Local storage: the app saves calculator history and preferences (theme, language, analytics choice) in your browser using localStorage.
  • Analytics (optional): only if you explicitly accept analytics inside the app.

2) What we do NOT collect

  • No passports / names / emails / phone numbers are requested by the app.
  • No hidden scraping of your Telegram chats or contacts.
  • No sale of personal data.

3) Analytics (opt-in)

If you accept analytics, we may collect aggregated usage events (e.g., which screen is opened, button clicks, errors). This is used to improve stability and UX. If you decline, the calculator still works.

4) Third-party services

The app may communicate with third parties from your device:

  • Telegram WebApp (for in-app UI integration).
  • TON Connect (wallet connection). If you connect a wallet, the wallet address can be shown inside the app and to the wallet provider.
  • Market/indicator APIs (e.g., for Fear & Greed and global market metrics). These calls are made from your device; those providers may see your IP address and user-agent.

5) Wallet & transactions (TON)

Wallet connection is initiated by you. We do not custody funds. Transactions are created and signed in your wallet. Network fees and wallet/provider rules apply.

6) Data retention

  • Local data stays on your device until you clear the app history or browser data.
  • Analytics data (if enabled) is stored according to the analytics provider’s retention settings.

7) Security

We use HTTPS hosting. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security. Do not share sensitive information via third-party links.

8) Your choices

  • You can decline analytics in the consent dialog.
  • You can remove local history via the “Clear” button (History) and by clearing browser storage.
  • You can disconnect TON wallet at any time.

9) Contact

Questions or requests: @Trader_TradeSupportBot
